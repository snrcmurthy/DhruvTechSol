Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical AI project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. A focused automation project might take 6-12 weeks, while comprehensive AI transformation initiatives typically span 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during the strategy phase.

Do I need to have data scientists on staff?

No. We handle the technical implementation and can train your existing team to maintain the systems we build. Many of our clients have no in-house AI expertise when they start working with us.

What industries do you work with?

We work across industries including healthcare, finance, manufacturing, retail, and professional services. Our approach focuses on understanding your specific business context rather than applying generic industry templates.

How do you ensure data security?

We follow industry-standard security protocols including encryption, access controls, and compliance with relevant regulations (GDPR, HIPAA, etc.). Security and privacy are built into our solutions from day one, not added as afterthoughts.
