## 🔴 MUST HAVE (Required by Law)

### 1. Privacy Policy ⚠️ REQUIRED

**Why:** Required by GDPR, CCPA, and most privacy laws worldwide
**What it covers:**
- What data you collect (names, emails, phone numbers, IP addresses)
- How you use the data
- Who you share data with (Formspree, Google reCAPTCHA, Vercel)
- User rights (access, deletion, opt-out)
- Cookie usage
- Data retention periods
- Contact information for privacy inquiries

**Action Required:** Create a Privacy Policy page

## 🚨 THINGS TO AVOID (Legal Risks)

### ❌ Don't Do This:

1. **False Claims**
   - Don't guarantee specific results
   - Don't claim certifications you don't have
   - Don't use fake testimonials or case studies

2. **Copyright Infringement**
   - Don't use stock photos without license ✅ (You're using CSS art)
   - Don't copy competitor content
   - Don't use trademarked terms improperly

3. **Misleading Marketing**
   - Don't make unsubstantiated claims
   - Don't use deceptive pricing
   - Don't hide fees or terms

4. **Data Misuse**
   - Don't sell user data without consent
   - Don't share data with unauthorized parties
   - Don't keep data longer than necessary

5. **Unlicensed Practice**
   - Don't practice law (legal advice)
   - Don't practice medicine (medical advice)
   - Don't provide financial advice without license
